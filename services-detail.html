River Acre Farm

River Acre Farm is a leisure farm project under Agrosage-Tek Uganda Limited located on a 5-acre land at 0.230362, 32.824278 (0°13'49.3"N 32°49'27.4" E) in Mukono District in Central Region, Uganda. It was established to enable young agricultural enthusiasts to meet, ideate, prototype and test their ambitious, bold, and innovative ideas, some of which develop into fully functional innovations with real-life applications that could benefit many farmers. It also serves as a demonstration farm where ATUL educators meet and practically trains local farmers in agricultural skills, including efficient modern food production techniques, climate-smart farming, post-harvest food handling, post-harvest food preservation, market research, record keeping, data management and

finance management. Ongoing projects include modern fish farming, energy-effective farm irrigation practices, organic farming techniques using indigenous micro-organisms manure (IMO), and cold storage food preservation techniques using the solar-powered air conditioner-operated walk-in cold room (Spacom-1). At River Acre Farm, we believe that those who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones that do. We are convinced that no dream is too big, and no dreamer is too small.
