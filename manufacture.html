Manufacturing food and preservation technology

The primary mandate of Agrosage-Tek is to design, test and manufacture food preservation technologies that are affordable, effective, reliable and environmentally friendly. We study the livelihood of the people whose lives we want to transform and use the results to design products most suitable and feasible to their way of life. Our first product is the Solar powered air conditioner operated walk-in cold room, a.k.a Spacom-1. Spacom-1 can preserve at least half a ton of desired food products such as fruits, vegetables, fish, meat, dairy products and even medicine. The product is manufactured within two months after an order is placed, and each unit is customised to suit best the intended use as described by the client.
