Hands-on training of farmers.

Our mission is to eradicate hunger and mitigate poverty among rural farmers through post-harvest loss reduction by providing them with affordable, effective, reliable and environmentally friendly food preservation technology. In order to achieve economic growth through post-harvest loss reduction, farmers must possess good agricultural knowledge that can guarantee the production of good quality products in the first place. Therefore, we establish free hands-on training at our River Acre Demonstration Farm in Mpumu, where farmers learn modern agricultural skills and also get a chance to share advice with one another.

Training is offered in areas such as pre-planting soil preparation, effective manure application, efficient pesticide usage, effective irrigation system installation, effective food harvesting, post-harvest food handling, post-harvest food preservation, market research, record keeping, data management and finance management.
